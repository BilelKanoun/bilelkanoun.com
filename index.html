Bilel Kanoun

He is currently a research engineer at Huawei Technologies R&D Center (ISP Team).
He was a post-doctoral researcher in the field of machine learning at Geoazur and Inria.
He was working on a project entitled:
"Extraction of curvilinear structure networks in image data using an innovative deep learning approach:
application to fracture and fault network extraction from satellite data"
The project has been funded by UCA-AI and is in the framework of both the Faults_R_Gems project
and Idex UCA-Jedi (Academy of Excellence).
